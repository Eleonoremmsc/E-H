Attire

Dress Code

Black Tie

We invite our guests to embrace the elegance of a Provençal evening. Formal attire is warmly encouraged.

For Women

  • Floor-length or midi evening gown
  • Elegant cocktail dress
  • Refined trouser suit

Soft tones, florals, and warm whites are all welcome. The ceremony is outdoors — block heels or wedges are recommended.

For Men

  • Black tie (dinner jacket & black tie)
  • Dark suit with tie

A dark suit with a tie is perfectly appropriate. Bow ties, pocket squares, and cummerbunds are all encouraged.

Please avoid white, ivory, and cream. Children are welcome in their finest.
